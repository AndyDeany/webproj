Aim Rule 26 Compliance

  • You can find a description of our business on our About Us page and a list of our key personnel on the Our People page (including the names of the Holmes Investment Properties Plc Company Directors their biographies and responsibilities)
  • Holmes Investment Properties Plc is not an Investing Company within the definition of the AIM rules;
  • Holmes Investment Properties Plc is a company incorporated in England;
  • The company mainly operates in England although we have sites in both Wales and Scotland;
  • You can find our current constitutional documents on the Articles of Association page
  • Holmes Investment Properties Plc is solely listed on the AIM and not on any other trading platform
  • You can find details of our securities not in public hands on our Shareholders page and we provide regular updates of our total shares in issue via the London Stock Exchange and on our RNS News feed page
  • There are no restrictions on the transfer of any Holmes Investment Properties Plc AIM securities;
  • All Annual Reports and Half Yearly reports can be found on the Annual Reports page and you can view our Investor Presentations on the Investors Presentations page.
  • The AIM admission document can be found on the AIM Rule 26 Compliance page
  • Holmes Investment Properties Plc is subject to the UK City Code on takeovers and mergers.
